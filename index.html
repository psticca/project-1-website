About Me

Hello, my name is Peter Sticca. Also known as ‘SpeCla‘. I am a Computer Science student at Murdoch University, computer enthusiast and avid gamer who is very competitive.

Currently, my main game is Playerunknown’s Battlegrounds, but over the last couple years, I have also achieved well above average rankings in Overwatch, Battlefield 1, World of Warcraft and League of Legends.

I’m an all round gamer who enjoys all types of games, but the FPS genre appeals to me the most as it is usually very competitive.

I am currently employed at Secure Computer Recycling and Disposal (SCRD) and have been in the company for 6 years. I learnt to build desktop computers from a young age and over the years have honed my skills. I can safely claim to have built over 100 computers for friends and family, within all price ranges. Basically I have become my friends and families – IT guy. Soon to come, a full custom water-loop in my desktop computer.
